FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before your arrival date. Cancellations within 7 days may incur a one-night charge. Contact us for specifics.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes! We can arrange transfers from Roman Tmetuchl International Airport. Contact our team when making your booking to coordinate pickup.

How do I pay for my booking?

We accept PayPal and credit/debit card payments via Stripe. A deposit may be required to confirm your reservation.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome well-behaved pets in select rooms. Please notify us at booking so we can arrange appropriate accommodations.

What activities can you arrange?

Our team can arrange snorkeling, diving, island tours, kayaking, and cultural experiences. Ask us about current packages!
