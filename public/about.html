Our Story

Our History

VIP Guest Hotel was founded with a vision to create a welcoming, authentic, family-run retreat right in the heart of Koror — the beating center of Palau's tourism industry. The hotel was built on the belief that travelers deserved more than just a place to sleep; they deserved a home away from home, where every face is familiar and every detail is personal.

Starting with just a handful of rooms, the hotel quickly earned a reputation among travelers for its warmth and exceptional service. Word spread through TripAdvisor reviews, Instagram posts, and returning guests who brought their families back year after year. Over time, the hotel grew — adding more room types, expanding amenities, and deepening its roots in the Palauan community — all while staying true to its family-owned character.

Today, VIP Guest Hotel proudly hosts guests from across the globe, offering comfortable rooms, local knowledge, and that irreplaceable family touch that only a true Palauan family-run hotel can provide.
